About Organization

The Health Promotion Foundation (HPF) is a non-profit non-governmental organization established in 2023 AD, in Chitwan District with a vision of creating a healthy society where every individual will have the knowledge, resources and support that they need to live a healthy and prosperous life. The organization is committed to promoting health and well-being through various programs and initiatives that focus on health education, disease prevention, and health promotion. HPF works closely with local communities, government agencies, and other stakeholders to identify health needs and develop effective strategies to address them.

The organization is dedicated to empowering individuals to make positive choices in their lives to prevent illness and promote their health. HPF is always working to promote health and wellness through education, advocacy and community-building initiatives.

The mission of the Health Promotion Foundation is to improve people’s health and well-being by promoting healthy behaviors, preventing diseases, and addressing social determinants of health, this may involve conducting research, developing and implementing health promotion programs, advocating for policies that support health and wellness, and collaborating with other organization and stakeholders to create a healthier community. Ultimately, the goal of the Health Promotion Foundation is to empower individuals and communities to make positive choices that lead to better health outcomes.

Our Thematic Areas

1. Lifestyle Modification

2. Prevention of disease and Promotion of Health

3. Non-communicable diseases related activities

4. Nutrition, water and sanitation, tropical diseases, childhood illness and safe motherhood

5. Climate change, agricultural health and disaster management

Our History

Health Promotion Foundation (HPF) was established in 2023 A.D. in Chitwan district of Nepal. The organization was founded by a group of health professionals and community leaders who recognized the need for a dedicated organization to promote health and well-being in the local community. The founders of HPF were motivated by a shared vision of creating a healthier society where individuals have access to the knowledge, resources, and support they need to live healthy lives. They believed that by working together with local communities, government agencies, and other stakeholders, they could make a positive impact on the health outcomes of the population. Since its establishment, HPF has been actively involved in various health promotion initiatives and programs, and has built a strong reputation as a trusted organization in the field of public health.

Over the years, HPF has expanded its reach and impact, and has become a leading organization in the field of health promotion in Nepal. The organization has received recognition for its innovative programs and initiatives, and has established partnerships with various national level organizations to further its mission of promoting health and well-being. Today, HPF continues to be driven by its founding principles of community empowerment, collaboration, and innovation, and remains committed to creating a healthier society for all.
