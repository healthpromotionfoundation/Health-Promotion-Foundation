Our Activities

We believe we can make a difference

Prevention of Disease and Promotion of Health

We focus on reducing risk factors, encouraging healthy lifestyles, and creating supportive environments.

Lifestyle Modification

Lifestyle modification involves making positive changes in daily habits such as eating a balanced diet, engaging in regular physical activity, managing stress, ensuring adequate sleep, and avoiding harmful practices like smoking or excessive alcohol consumption.

Climate change and agricultural health

We focuses on safe and sustainable farming practices, ensuring food security, reducing the spread of pests and diseases, and protecting both farmers’ and consumers’ health.”

Awareness and Health Education

Non-communicable diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, cancer, and chronic respiratory illnesses are major public health challenges. Activities to address NCDs include health education, lifestyle modification, regular screening, early diagnosis, promoting healthy diets, encouraging physical activity, controlling tobacco and alcohol use, and ensuring access to quality care and medicines.
